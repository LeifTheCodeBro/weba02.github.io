Positions

At Archi.Techt, we offer a wide range of postitions that you can play a roll in. Here's a list of the positions we have available. And if any of these interest you, then please don't be afraid to contact us.

Positions Availablility Description
Computer Technician Yes As a computer technician, your main job will be to handle the compter than seemingly have issues with them, and to fix those issues. It's a very rewarding job that shows well on your resume.
Customer Service No Your main responsibilities as someone part of the custumer service crew, will be to have friendly conversations with customers and help them through their issue and if you can't fix it, then help them get the help they need. It's a job that can sometimes be frustrating because technologie is not everyone's strong suit, so you'll have to also be quite patient.
Cashier Yes You will be working the cash registers but you'll be tought some of the basics of computer hardware parts, and overall computer knowledge so that when the time comes, you'll be able to help the customer without always needing to send them to customer service.
General Maintence Yes Your job will be to make sure the floors, the back room, the aisles, and the bathrooms are clean and tidy.
Store Manager No The store manager's job is to look over the runnings of the store when the owner is out. You'll also be tasked with recording our stock and making sure if we need to order anything, then to do that. Other than that, your main responsibilities include, making sure everyone is doing their job and isn't slacking off. Making sure that all the customers are satisfied and if they aren't, then to do your best to make sure they leave in a more positive mood.
